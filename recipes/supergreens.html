Daddies Super Greens

Super Greens a tasty fast veggie meal

Super Greens is an easy and refreshing side dish, inspired by a Panda Express favorite. It features broccoli, cabbage, and kale, all steamed to a crisp-tender perfection and tossed in a light garlic-ginger sauce. This savory yet vibrant dish is sure to become a regular on your menu!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons minced fresh garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 3 cups medium sized broccoli florets
  • 3 cups chopped green cabbage leaves in 1-2” pieces
  • 3 cups chopped curly kale leaves in 1-2” pieces (discard tough stems)
  • ¼ cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • ½ tablespoon rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add butter and allow it to melt
  2. Stir in garlic and ginger. Cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, cabbage, kale, and vegetable broth. Fold to combine. Cover skillet with a lid and steam the vegetables for 2 minutes. Lift the lid and quickly fold the greens a couple times, then cover and steam for 2 more minutes or until crisp-tender and bright green. The vegetables will shrink down considerably in size.
  4. Add soy sauce and rice vinegar. Stir to combine, sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired, and serve immediately.
